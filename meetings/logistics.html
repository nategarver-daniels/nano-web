Internet Access

There will be no wireless internet access on the observatory campus. You can access the internet using ethernet cords found in your room and in the meeting room. If you don’t have an ethernet port on your laptop, remember to bring an adapter. There will be nowhere to buy one there.

Meals

Breakfast (7:30am) and lunch (12:30pm) will be held at the Learning Center each morning. Dinner (5:30 pm) will be held at the cafeteria. The conference dinner on Wednesday evening will be held at 6 pm by the pool. If you’re arriving on Sunday, you’ll need to get dinner on the way to the observatory.

Room Changes

If you will be changing rooms in the middle of the conference, your belongings will need to be removed by 9 am. If you need any help storing your belongings, please contact Megan Moore (megan.moore@mail.wvu.edu).

Packing

The weather will be warm during the day (80s) but can dip to 50s or 60s at night. Bring warm clothes for night-time and for the conference rooms, since they are kept quite cold. There is a pool, so bring a bathing suit. Also, bring comfortable walking shoes as there are hills and rough pathways on site.

Payment

All participants will be charged $20/day; this will cover all meals and snacks aside from the conference dinner, which will be paid for by the PIRE. There is no need to pay for meals individually. If you’ll be paying for your lodging and meals yourself, you can pay upon check-out or an invoice will be sent to you after the meeting. If your institution will be paying for your lodging and meals, the institution will receive an invoice after the meeting. If you are a student, please check with your advisor before paying for rooming or meals.

Phones

Your cell phone may or may not work in PR; check with your provider. The observatory number is 787-878-2612 if someone needs to get in touch with you.

Questions?

If you have any questions or requests during the meeting, please contact Megan Moore (megan.moore@mail.wvu.edu or 843-455-8746) or Maura McLaughlin (maura.mclaughlin@mail.wvu.edu or 304-290-0416). Note that these cell phone numbers will work outside of the observatory only.
